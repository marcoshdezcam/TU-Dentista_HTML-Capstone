TU Dentista works in four simple steps:

Search

It's simple. On the TU Dentista homepage, enter the name of a doctor, specialty, or a keyword of something you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all schools.

Locate

Get to see where the dentist is located by clicking on the dentist's profile. And you can get directions from anywhere in the country.

Make an appointment

Call your favorite dentist and make an appointment with them. Click on the call button.

Rate

Rate the experience you had with them and help others to find the best dentist near them too!
